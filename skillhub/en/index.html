Welcome to AI Store Labs

Getting Started

Welcome to the AI-Powered-Store training platform. This SkillHub provides interactive lessons covering every aspect of the platform — from installation to advanced GPU management.

How to Use SkillHub

Navigate through lessons using the sidebar on the left. Lessons are organized by difficulty level:

  • Beginner — Platform setup and basic operations
  • Intermediate — Application lifecycle and data management
  • Advanced — GPU workloads, serverless, and billing

Tracking Your Progress

As you complete each lesson, click the "Mark as Complete" button. Your progress is saved locally and displayed in the header progress bar.

Tip: Each lesson has an accompanying hands-on lab. Complete the reading first, then launch the lab for practical exercises.
